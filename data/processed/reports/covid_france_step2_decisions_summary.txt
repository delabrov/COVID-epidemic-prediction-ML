3) Decisions Locked for Next Stage
----------------------------------
Decision 1 - Study interval
- Use only: 2020-01-05 -> 2023-07-01
Rationale:
- End of full series contains reporting gaps and suspicious plateaus, not suitable for dynamic modeling.

Decision 2 - Main incidence signal for modeling
- Use new_cases_7d_avg as primary case signal for SIR and future ML.
Rationale:
- 7-day smoothing stabilizes daily reporting artifacts and aligns with epidemiological interpretation.

Decision 3 - Keep population normalizations
- Keep and use normalized variables (per 100k and percent of population).
Rationale:
- Improves interpretability and comparability over time and for future multi-region extension.
